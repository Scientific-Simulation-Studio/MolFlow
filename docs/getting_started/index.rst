Getting started
===============

Welcome to molflow!

Please follow the :ref:`/getting_started/deployment.rst#deployment` and :ref:`getting_started/installation:installation` documentation to get molflow ready.

Then, we encourage you to check :ref:`tutorial` to play with molflow.

After that, please check :ref:`components` which are first-class tools and utilities to facilitate your development.

Finally, please refer to :ref:`developer` and :ref:`api` when you write you own molflow programs.
